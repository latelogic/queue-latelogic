Our Mission

To eliminate the uncertainty of airport travel.

AirportDelay (formerly FlightQueue) was born from a simple frustration: missing a flight due to an unexpectedly long security line.

We realized that while flight tracking apps were abundant, reliable information about what happens *inside* the airport was scarce. Security checkpoints, immigration halls, and check-in counters are often black holes of information.

Our team of data scientists and travel enthusiasts built a platform that aggregates real-time data from official sources, social signals, and user reports to give you the most accurate wait time predictions available.

Today, we help thousands of travelers plan their airport arrival time with confidence.
